Jingrui NIU

Postdoctoral researcher

Laboratoire Jacques-Louis Lions, Sorbonne Université

I am currently a post-doc at Laboratoire Jacques Louis-Lions under the supervision of Ugo Boscain and Mario Sigalotti. I am also a member of the Inria Team CAGE and Inria Team McTAO. I obtained my Ph.D. degree at Université Paris-Saclay under the supervision of Nicolas Burq and Pierre Lissy.

Interests
  • Dispersive equations
  • Microlocal Analysis
  • Controllability and Stabilization of dispersive equations and coupled systems
  • Control of quantum mechanical systems
